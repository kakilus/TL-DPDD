🇬🇧 UK Weather Facts & Figures

🌡️ Average Annual Temperature

2022 was the UK's warmest year on record, with a mean temperature of 10.03°C, surpassing the previous record of 9.88°C in 2014.

England recorded the highest average at 10.94°C, followed by Wales at 10.23°C, Northern Ireland at 9.85°C, and Scotland at 8.50°C.

🌧️ Average Annual Rainfall

The UK averages approximately 1,154 mm of rainfall annually.

The wettest areas include Snowdonia, the Lake District, and the Scottish Highlands, with some regions receiving over 4,000 mm of rain per year.

☀️ Average Annual Sunshine Hours

The UK averages about 1,403 hours of sunshine annually.

Weymouth in Southern England is among the sunniest locations, with approximately 1,904 hours of sunshine per year.

🌡️ Recent Climate Trends

The summer of 2025 was officially the warmest on record in the UK, driven by unusually warm coastal sea temperatures and a prolonged lack of rain.

The UK has experienced its hottest and sunniest spring on record in 2025, with a mean temperature of 9.5°C and 653.3 hours of sunshine, 43% above average.
